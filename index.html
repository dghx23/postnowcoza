COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the return process work?
You choose at upload time: Option A (Direct Return) uses a prepaid courier sticker for fast collection back to you. Option B (Fully Managed) routes the signed document back to our secure facility for scanning, encrypted archival, and optional certified destruction with a formal certificate.
Is PostNow POPIA compliant?
Yes. The entire platform and all processes were designed with POPIA as a core requirement. We maintain full chain-of-custody records, access controls, and support data subject rights (access, deletion, etc.).
What happens to the original document after signing?
It depends on your chosen return pathway. With Option B you can request professional destruction and receive a Certificate of Destruction linked to the immutable audit log. Option A returns the original directly to you.
Can I track my document in real time?
Yes. Every document has a dedicated tracking page with live courier status, status timeline, and the full chain-of-custody log. You can share the link with clients or colleagues.
How long does delivery usually take?
Most urban deliveries are completed within 1–2 business days. Remote and rural areas may take 2–4 days. All shipments are tracked end-to-end.
Do you support high-volume or bulk dispatches?
Yes. The platform is built for both individual high-value documents and higher-volume workflows. Contact us for enterprise or volume pricing.
